Vision

As we frequently observe, some animals perish as a result of the animals being in such poor condition on the sides of roads. To ensure that these animals receive proper care, the Doon animal welfare organisation was established. These animals receive not just medical care but also food, medication, a caring atmosphere, and other necessities for survival at Doon Animal Welfare. The staff at Doon Animal Welfare inspires and motivates society to assist the living when we confront such circumstances where these animals have survived in cruel conditions. Why do we put these animals in conditions where they suffer so much when they have the same right to have a respectable life as other living beings? The domesticated animals, including bulls, buffaloes, and others, are housed in this charitable organisation so they won't have to endure harsh conditions.

History of DAW

In order to help the helpless creatures, the Doon Animal Welfare was introduced in Dehradun in 2016 and successfully registered in Dehradun NGO'S. The head office of this nonprofit organisation is located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. This nonprofit organisation has an active status and is a private limited company. The Doon Animal Welfare was founded with the intention of offering food, medicine, shelter, and other essentials while continuously improving its offerings. We are pleased to note that the development of this foundation has empowered numerous animals to acquire their own shelter. As the words of the founder of Doon Animal Welfare MILLI KAUR that once someone told her to do something great and blissful with the motivation of those words I am blessed with this organisation which is proven as a miracle to alot animals

Values and Mission

We at Doon Animal welfare,

Doon Animal welfare, moves forward with a mission to improve community effect for the voiceless, innocent creatures. We are moving on with the intention to provide 5000 animals sanctuary in the future years.
